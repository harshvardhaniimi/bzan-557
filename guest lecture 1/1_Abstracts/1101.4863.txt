An infinite family of convex Brunnian links in $R^n$;;; Robert Davis,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Howards_H/0/1/0/all/0/1">Hugh Howards</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Newman_J/0/1/0/all/0/1">Jonathan Newman</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Parsley_J/0/1/0/all/0/1">Jason Parsley</a> ;;; This paper proves that convex Brunnian links exist for every dimension $n \geq 3$ by constructing explicit examples. These examples are three-component links which are higher-dimensional generalizations of the Borromean rings.
