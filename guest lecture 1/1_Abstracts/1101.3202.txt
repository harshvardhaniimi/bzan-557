New examples of defective secant varieties of Segre-Veronese varieties;;; Hirotachi Abo,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Brambilla_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Maria Chiara Brambilla</a> ;;; We prove the existence of defective secant varieties of three-factor and four-factor Segre-Veronese varieties embedded in certain multi-degree. These defective secant varieties were previously unknown and are of importance in the classification of defective secant varieties of Segre-Veronese varieties with three or more factors.
