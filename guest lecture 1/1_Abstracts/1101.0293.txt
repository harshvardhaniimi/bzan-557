Categorification of the polynomial ring;;; Mikhail Khovanov,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Sazdanovic_R/0/1/0/all/0/1">Radmila Sazdanovic</a> ;;; We develop a diagrammatic categorification of the polynomial ring $Z[x]$. Our categorification satisfies a version of Bernstein-Gelfand-Gelfand reciprocity property with the indecomposable projective modules corresponding to $x^n$ and standard modules to $(x-1)^n$ in the Grothendieck ring.
