Zero free regions for Dirichlet series;;; Christophe Delaunay, (ICJ),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Fricain_E/0/1/0/all/0/1">Emmanuel Fricain</a> (ICJ),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Mosaki_E/0/1/0/all/0/1">Elie Mosaki</a> (ICJ),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Robert_O/0/1/0/all/0/1">Olivier Robert</a> ;;; In this paper, we are interested in explicit zero-free discs for some Dirichlet series and we also study a general Beurling-Nyman criterion for $L$-functions. Our results generalize and improve previous results obtained by N. Nikolski and by A. de Roton. As a concrete application, we get, for example, a Beurling-Nyman type criterion for the Siegel zero problem.
