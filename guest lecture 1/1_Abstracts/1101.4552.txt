Suppleness of the sheaf of algebras of generalized functions on  manifolds;;; Stevan Pilipovic,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Zigic_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Milica Zigic</a> ;;; We show that the sheaves of algebras of generalized functions $\Omega\to \mathcal{G}(\Omega)$ and $\Omega\to \mathcal{G}^{\infty}(\Omega)$, $\Omega$ are open sets in a manifold $X$, are supple, contrary to the non-suppleness of the sheaf of distributions.
