On one extremal property of a regular simplex;;; Vladislav Babenko,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Babenko_Y/0/1/0/all/0/1">Yuliya Babenko</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Parfinovych_N/0/1/0/all/0/1">Nataliya Parfinovych</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Skorokhodov_D/0/1/0/all/0/1">Dmytro Skorokhodov</a> ;;; In this paper, we show that the $L_p$-error of asymmetric linear approximation of the quadratic function $Q({\mathbf x})=\sum_{j=1}^{d}x_j^2$ on simplices in $\RR^d$ of fixed volume is minimized on regular simplices.
