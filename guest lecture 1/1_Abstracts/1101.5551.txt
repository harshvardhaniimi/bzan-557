The Clifford deformation of the Hermite semigroup;;; H. De Bie,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Orsted_B/0/1/0/all/0/1">B. Orsted</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Somberg_P/0/1/0/all/0/1">P. Somberg</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Soucek_V/0/1/0/all/0/1">V. Soucek</a> ;;; This paper is a continuation of the paper [H. De Bie et al., Dunkl operators and a family of realizations of osp(1|2), <a href="/abs/0911.4725">arXiv:0911.4725</a>], investigating a natural radial deformation of the Fourier transform in the setting of Clifford analysis. At the same time, it gives extensions of many results obtained in [S. Ben Said et al., Laguerre semigroup and Dunkl operators, <a href="/abs/0907.3749">arXiv:0907.3749</a>]. We establish the analogues of Bochner's formula and the Heisenberg uncertainty relation in the framework of the (holomorphic) Hermite semigroup, and also give a detailed analytic treatment of the series expansion of the associated integral transform.
