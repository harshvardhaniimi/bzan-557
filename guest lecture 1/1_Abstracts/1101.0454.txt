Conformal flatness, non-Abelian Kaluza-Klein and the Quaternions;;; Paolo Maraner,,  <a href="/find/math-ph,math/1/au:+Pachos_J/0/1/0/all/0/1">Jiannis K. Pachos</a> ;;; Maximally symmetric manifolds with holonomy in the unitary quaternionic group Sp(d/4) emerge from the non-Abelian Kaluza-Klein reduction of conformally flat spaces. Thus, all special manifolds with constant properly `holonomy-related' sectional curvature, are in natural correspondence with conformally flat, possibly non-Abelian, Kaluza-Klein spaces.
