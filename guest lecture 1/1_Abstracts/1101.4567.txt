On a classical limit of q-deformed Whittaker functions;;; Anton Gerasimov,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Lebedev_D/0/1/0/all/0/1">Dimitri Lebedev</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Oblezin_S/0/1/0/all/0/1">Sergey Oblezin</a> ;;; We provide a derivation of the Givental integral representation of the classical $gl_{\ell+1}$-Whittaker function as a limit $q \to 1$ of the q-deformed $gl_{\ell+1}$-Whittaker function represented as a sum over the Gelfand-Zetlin patterns.
