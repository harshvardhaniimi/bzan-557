Proof of some conjectures of Z.-W. Sun on congruences for Apery  polynomials;;; Victor J. W. Guo,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Zeng_J/0/1/0/all/0/1">Jiang Zeng</a> ;;; The Apery polynomials are defined by $A_n(x)=\sum_{k=0}^{n}{n\choose k}^2{n+k\choose k}^2 x^k$ for all nonnegative integers $n$. We confirm several conjectures of Z.-W. Sun on the congruences for the sum $\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}(-1)^k(2k+1) A_k(x)$ with $x\in Z$.
