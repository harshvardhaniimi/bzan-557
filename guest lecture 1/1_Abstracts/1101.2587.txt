Radial projections of rectifiable sets;;; Tuomas Orponen,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Sahlsten_T/0/1/0/all/0/1">Tuomas Sahlsten</a> ;;; We show that if no $m$-plane contains almost all of an $m$-rectifiable set $E \subset \R^{n}$, then there exists a single $(m - 1)$-plane $V$ such that the radial projection of $E$ has positive $m$-dimensional measure from every point outside $V$.
