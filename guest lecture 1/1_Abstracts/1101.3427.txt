Doubly-refined enumeration of Alternating Sign Matrices and determinants  of 2-staircase Schur functions;;; Philippe Biane,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Cantini_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Luigi Cantini</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Sportiello_A/0/1/0/all/0/1">Andrea Sportiello</a> ;;; We prove a determinantal identity concerning Schur functions for 2-staircase diagrams lambda=(ln+l',ln,l(n-1)+l',l(n-1),...,l+l',l,l',0). When l=1 and l'=0 these functions are related to the partition function of the 6-vertex model at the combinatorial point and hence to enumerations of Alternating Sign Matrices. A consequence of our result is an identity concerning the doubly-refined enumerations of Alternating Sign Matrices.
