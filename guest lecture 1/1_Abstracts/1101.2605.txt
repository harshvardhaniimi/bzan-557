Riordan arrays and the LDU decomposition of symmetric Toeplitz plus  Hankel matrices;;; Paul Barry,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Hennessy_A/0/1/0/all/0/1">Aoife Hennessy</a> ;;; We examine a result of Basor and Ehrhardt concerning Hankel and Toeplitz plus Hankel matrices, within the context of the Riordan group of lower-triangular matrices. This allows us to determine the LDU decomposition of certain symmetric Toeplitz plus Hankel matrices. We also determine the generating functions and Hankel transforms of associated sequences.
