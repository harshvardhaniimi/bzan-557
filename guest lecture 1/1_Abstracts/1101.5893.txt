On reflection subgroups of finite Coxeter groups;;; J. Matthew Douglass,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Pfeiffer_G/0/1/0/all/0/1">Goetz Pfeiffer</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Roehrle_G/0/1/0/all/0/1">Gerhard Roehrle</a> ;;; Let $W$ be a finite Coxeter group. We classify the reflection subgroups of $W$ up to conjugacy and give necessary and sufficient conditions for the map that assigns to a reflection subgroup $R$ of $W$ the conjugacy class of its Coxeter elements to be injective, up to conjugacy.
