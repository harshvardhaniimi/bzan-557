Generalized Hamilton&#x27;s Principle with Fractional Derivatives;;; Teodor M. Atanackovic,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Konjik_S/0/1/0/all/0/1">Sanja Konjik</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Oparnica_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Ljubica Oparnica</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Pilipovic_S/0/1/0/all/0/1">Stevan Pilipovic</a> ;;; We generalize Hamilton's principle with fractional derivatives in Lagrangian $L(t,y(t),{}_0D_t^\al y(t),\alpha)$ so that the function $y$ and the order of fractional derivative $\alpha$ are varied in the minimization procedure. We derive stationarity conditions and discuss them through several examples.
