On Stratonovich and Skorohod stochastic calculus for Gaussian processes;;; Samy Tindel, (IECN),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Jolis_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Maria Jolis</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Hu_Y/0/1/0/all/0/1">Yaozhong Hu</a> ;;; In this article, we derive a Stratonovich and Skorohod type change of variables formula for a multidimensional Gaussian process with low H\"older regularity (typically lower than 1/4). To this aim, we combine tools from rough paths theory and stochastic analysis.
