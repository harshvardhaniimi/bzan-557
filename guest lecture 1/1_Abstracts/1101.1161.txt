The first returning speed and the last exit speed of a type of Markov  chain;;; Huizeng Zhang,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+zhao_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Minzhi zhao</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+wang_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Lei wang</a> ;;; Let $\{X_n\}$ be a Markov chain with transition probability $p_{ij}=a_{j-(i-1)^+},\forall i,j\ge 0$, where $a_j=0$ provided $j&lt;0$, $a_0&gt;0$, $a_0+a_1&lt;1$ and $\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n=1$. Let $\mu=\sum_{n=1}^\infty na_n$. It's known that $\{X_n\}$ is positive recurrent when $\mu&lt;1$; is null recurrent when $\mu=1$; and is transient when $\mu&gt;1$. In this paper, we shall discuss the first returning speed and the last exit speed more precisely by means of $\{a_n\}$
