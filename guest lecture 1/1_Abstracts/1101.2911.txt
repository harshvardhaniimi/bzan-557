Lelong classes on toric varieties and a theorem of Siciak;;; Maritza M. Branker,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Stawiska_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Malgorzata Stawiska</a> ;;; We characterize Lelong classes on a toric manifold with an ample torus invariant line bundle, generalizing an approximation theorem due to Siciak. We include a counterexample to the theorem when the line bundle is globally generated, but not ample.
