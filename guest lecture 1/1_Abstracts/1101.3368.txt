Ideals with Larger Projective Dimension and Regularity;;; Jesse Beder,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+McCullough_J/0/1/0/all/0/1">Jason McCullough</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Nunez_Betancourt_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Luis Nunez-Betancourt</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Seceleanu_A/0/1/0/all/0/1">Alexandra Seceleanu</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Snapp_B/0/1/0/all/0/1">Bart Snapp</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Stone_B/0/1/0/all/0/1">Branden Stone</a> ;;; We define a family of homogeneous ideals with large projective dimension and regularity relative to the number of generators and their common degree. This family subsumes and improves upon constructions given in [Cav04] and [McC]. In particular, we describe a family of three-generated homogeneous ideals in arbitrary characteristic whose projective dimension grows asymptotically as sqrt{d}^(sqrt(d) - 1).
