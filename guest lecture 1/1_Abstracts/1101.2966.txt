Waves in fractional Zener type viscoelastic media;;; Sanja Konjik,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Oparnica_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Ljubica Oparnica</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Zorica_D/0/1/0/all/0/1">Du\vsan Zorica</a> ;;; Classical wave equation is generalized for the case of viscoelastic materials obeying fractional Zener model instead of Hooke's law. Cauchy problem for such an equation is studied: existence and uniqueness of the fundamental solution is proven and solution is calculated.
