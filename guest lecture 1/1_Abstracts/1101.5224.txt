An isoperimetric inequality for eigenvalues of the bi-harmonic operator;;; Q. Ding,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Feng_G/0/1/0/all/0/1">G. Feng</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Zhang_Y/0/1/0/all/0/1">Y. Zhang</a> ;;; } In this article, we put forward a Neumann eigenvalue problem for the bi-harmonic operator $\Delta^2$ on a bounded smooth domain $\Om$ in the Euclidean $n$-space ${\bf R}^n$ ($n\ge2$) and then prove that the corresponding first non-zero eigenvalue $\Upsilon_1(\Om)$ admits the isoperimetric inequality of Szeg\"o-Weinberger type: $\Upsilon_1(\Om)\le \Upsilon_1(B_{\Om})$, where $B_{\Om}$ is a ball in ${\bf R}^n$ with the same volume of $\Om$. The isoperimetric inequality of Szeg\"o-Weinberger type for the first nonzero Neumann eigenvalue of the even-multi-Laplacian operators $\Delta^{2m}$ ($m\ge1$) on $\Om$ is also exploited.
