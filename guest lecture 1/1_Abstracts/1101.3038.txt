Hunt&#x27;s hypothesis (H) and Getoor&#x27;s conjecture for L&#xe9;vy Processes;;; Ze-Chun Hu,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Sun_W/0/1/0/all/0/1">Wei Sun</a> ;;; In this paper, Hunt's hypothesis (H) and Getoor's conjecture for L\'{e}vy processes are revisited. Let $X$ be a L\'{e}vy process on $\mathbf{R}^n$ with L\'{e}vy-Khintchine exponent $(a,A,\mu)$. First, we show that if $A$ is non-degenerate then $X$ satisfies (H). Second, under the assumption that $\mu({\mathbf{R}^n\backslash \sqrt{A}\mathbf{R}^n})&lt;\infty$, we show that $X$ satisfies (H) if and only if the equation $$ \sqrt{A}y=-a-\int_{\{x\in {\mathbf{R}^n\backslash \sqrt{A}\mathbf{R}^n}:\,|x|&lt;1\}}x\mu(dx),\ y\in \mathbf{R}^n, $$ has at least one solution. Finally, we show that if $X$ is a subordinator and satisfies (H) then its drift coefficient must be 0.
