Second-order corrections to the non-commutative Klein-Gordon equation  with a Coulomb potential;;; Slimane Zaim,,  <a href="/find/math-ph,math/1/au:+Khodja_L/0/1/0/all/0/1">Lamine Khodja</a>,  <a href="/find/math-ph,math/1/au:+Delenda_Y/0/1/0/all/0/1">Yazid Delenda</a> ;;; We improve the previous study of the Klein-Gordon equation in a non-commutative space-time as applied to the Hydrogen atom to extract the energy levels, by considering the second-order corrections in the non-commutativity parameter. Phenomenologically we show that non-commutativity is the source of lamb shift corrections.
