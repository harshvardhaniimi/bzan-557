Existence and stability of weak solutions for a degenerate parabolic  system modelling two-phase flows in porous media;;; Joachim Escher, (IFAM),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Laurencot_P/0/1/0/all/0/1">Philippe Laurencot</a> (IMT),  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Matioc_B/0/1/0/all/0/1">Bogdan-Vasile Matioc</a> (IFAM) ;;; We prove global existence of nonnegative weak solutions to a degenerate parabolic system which models the interaction of two thin fluid films in a porous medium. Furthermore, we show that these weak solutions converge at an exponential rate towards flat equilibria.
