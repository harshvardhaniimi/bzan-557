Quantum tunneling on graphs;;; Yong Lin,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Lippner_G/0/1/0/all/0/1">Gabor Lippner</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Yau_S/0/1/0/all/0/1">Shing-Tung Yau</a> ;;; We explore the tunneling behavior of a quantum particle on a finite graph, in the presence of an asymptotically large potential. Surprisingly the behavior is governed by the local symmetry of the graph around the wells.
