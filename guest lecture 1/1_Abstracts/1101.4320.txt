Multi-norms and the injectivity of $L^p(G)$;;; H. Garth Dales,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Daws_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Matthew Daws</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Pham_H/0/1/0/all/0/1">Hung Le Pham</a>,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Ramsden_P/0/1/0/all/0/1">Paul Ramsden</a> ;;; Let $G$ be a locally compact group, and take $p\in(1,\infty)$. We prove that the Banach left $L^1(G)$-module $L^p(G)$ is injective (if and) only if the group $G$ is amenable. Our proof uses the notion of multi-norms. We also develop the theory of multi-normed spaces.
