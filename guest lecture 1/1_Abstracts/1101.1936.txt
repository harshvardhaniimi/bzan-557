Self-injective right artinian rings and Igusa Todorov functions;;; Fran\ccois Huard,,  <a href="/find/math/1/au:+Lanzilotta_M/0/1/0/all/0/1">Marcelo Lanzilotta</a> ;;; We show that a right artinian ring $R$ is right self-injective if and only if $\psi(M)=0$ (or equivalently $\phi(M)=0$) for all finitely generated right $R$-modules $M$, where $\psi, \phi : \mod R \to \mathbb N$ are functions defined by Igusa and Todorov. In particular, an artin algebra $\Lambda$ is self-injective if and only if $\phi(M)=0$ for all finitely generated right $\Lambda$-modules $M$.
